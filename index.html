BELLABEAT FITNESS TRACKER

BELLABEAT CASE STUDY- FITBIT FITNESS TRACKER ANALYSIS

CHECK IT OUT

Description

The Bellabeat case study analyzed the usage of Fitbit fitness tracker by the women-focused wellness company to gain insights into their customers' behavior patterns. The study collected data on sleep, activity, and stress levels of users and used it to improve Bellabeat's products and services. The analysis revealed that users who tracked their activities and sleep tend to have healthier lifestyles. This allowed Bellabeat to develop tailored wellness programs for their users, leading to an increase in customer satisfaction and engagement.

DEFORESTATION EXPLORATION

AN INSIGHTFUL ANALYSIS TO SUPPORT GLOBAL REFORESTATION EFFORTS

CHECK IT OUT

Description

Deforestation Exploration is a project aimed at reducing deforestation and raising awareness on environmental issues. The analysis provides a comprehensive overview of deforestation rates and trends by region and country.
The report includes actionable recommendations for ForestQuery to allocate resources and have the most significant impact.
The project is presented in five sections: Introduction, Instructions, Global Situation, Regional Outlook, and Country-Level Detail. The appendix includes SQL queries used for the analysis.
